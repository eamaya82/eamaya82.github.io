Pegaso - Software development
K2 Solutions TOC 2007 Team of 5 Developers

Development of the "Pegaso" project: Utilizing management paradigm of the theory of constraints (TOC) applied to logistics and inventory management in the distribution and supply chain systems, Pegaso manages and optimizes the inventory management following the alignments of the distribution chain that apply.


Pegaso Core TOC implementation:

  • Identify the constraint(s) of the supply chain.
  • Suggests how to deal with the constraint(s) of the system.
  • Delegation of tasks based on permits and authorization.
  • Warning system based on Buffers, "red line limit", days in zone among other system data.

Programmed to work in Microsoft environments and using client-server architecture, it guarantees the robustness necessary to process a high amount of data in a short time is tested to manage more than 10,000 references.
